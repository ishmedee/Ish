Хянадаггүй. Иш нь хэрэглэгчийн зан төлөв, үйлдлийг хянах, дагах технологи (cookie, tracker) ашигладаггүй.

Гуравдагч тал. Иш нь Facebook (Meta) платформ дээр ажилладаг тул Facebook-ийн өөрийн нууцлалын бодлого мөн үйлчилнэ.

In English

Иш (Ish) is an automated news service that collects news from publicly available Mongolian news sources and posts concise summaries to its Facebook Page.

No personal data collected. Ish does not collect, store, or share any personal information (name, email, phone, location, etc.) from users. It operates no account system or login.

No tracking. Ish does not use cookies, trackers, or any technology to monitor user behavior.

Sources. Every post cites its source and links back to it. All information is drawn from publicly available news sources.

Third parties. Ish operates on the Facebook (Meta) platform, so Facebook's own privacy policy also applies.
